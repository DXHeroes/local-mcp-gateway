# Customer Segments

Target customer analysis for Local MCP Gateway.

## Overview

Local MCP Gateway serves three primary customer segments:

1. **Individual Developers** - Personal use, hobbyists
2. **Startups & SMBs** - Small teams, growing companies
3. **Enterprise** - Large organizations, compliance needs

---

## Segment Overview

| Segment | Size | Priority | Revenue Potential |
|---------|------|----------|-------------------|
| Individual Developers | Large | High (adoption) | Low |
| Startups & SMBs | Medium | High (conversion) | Medium |
| Enterprise | Small | Medium | High |

## Acquisition Strategy

### Individual Developers

**Channels**:
- Developer blogs and tutorials
- GitHub presence
- Dev community (Reddit, HN, Discord)
- Conference talks

**Conversion Path**:
- Free tier → power user → Pro subscription

### Startups & SMBs

**Channels**:
- Product Hunt launch
- Startup accelerator partnerships
- LinkedIn ads
- Content marketing

**Conversion Path**:
- Free trial → Team subscription

### Enterprise

**Channels**:
- Enterprise sales team
- Partner referrals
- Industry conferences
- Case studies

**Conversion Path**:
- Demo → POC → Contract negotiation
